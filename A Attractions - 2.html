ATTRACTIONS:

 

Find a host of great things to do when you visit the Harrogate District, with some of the best known attractions in North Yorkshire from gardens to castles, adventure centres to art galleries there is something for every taste and interest, every hobby and passion.

All of the attractions offer family friendly themed activities meaning there is plenty to do for children of all ages. They now offer extensive play areas there are also interactive displays and activities where children can get hands on and explore the story of the house and grounds.

Millions of years of water erosion have left some amazing landmarks and features around the district. These offer great low cost things to do around Harrogate District such as picnic locations and beauty spots, but still with space to explore and enjoy the outdoors.

With all of these options, why would'nt you?
